Chicken Cheesesteak Pita Recipe

Description

The Pitas shown above melt in the mouth in a mixture of creamy cheese and savory meat. The dish is a perfect lunchtime meal to satiate a day's long hunger

Don't forget to savor this food, something had to sacrifice for it.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of shredded chicken
  • 1 cup of grated pepperjack cheese
  • 1/4 cups of diced onions
  • 1 handfull of jalepenos
  • 2 tbsp black olives
  • 2 flour wheat pitas

Steps

  1. Prepare diced,shredded, and minced ingredients mentioned above. Place 1 tbsp of oil into iron caskett above low lit flame.
  2. Mix materials, exclduing the cheese, within iron pan beofre turining up flame to medium for 5-10 minutes Ensure that ingredients are properly cripsed.
  3. Add cheese above cooked materials. Ensure that the cheese is distributed evenly. Place lid above iron pan until cheese is fully melted Use spatula to seperate cheesesteak into 2 even sections then place on pitas Add any preferred sauces on top of Pita.
  4. Enjoy :)
