Fruit Salad Recipe

Description

The Fruit Salad in the picture is more than it appears to be. Fruit is an essential food made for plant reproduction. Beyond this, the sweet and sour taste range introduces a world of health and flavor.

Respect the gift given because it is not only original but also fleeting.

Ingredients

  • Pineapple
  • Strawberries
  • Grapes
  • Watermelon
  • Cantaloupe
  • kiwi
  • Blueberries
  • Oranges

Steps

  1. Rinse fruit under warm water then place into seperate bowls
  2. Use a knife to cut the fruit into small pieces
  3. Place diced fruit into large Bowl Gently Mix fruit using spoon
  4. Place in a fridge for 20-30 minutes to chill
  5. Enjoy!!
