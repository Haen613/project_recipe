Sandwich Recipe

Description

The Sandwich above is the perfect break from a scorching summer day. The fresh taste of lettuce and tomato against spciy cheese and smooth turkey speaks for itself.

Remember a Sanwich is easier to make than a Sandwitch!

Ingredients

  • Boar-head Turkey
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • 2 slices of Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pepperjack cheese
  • Condiments
  • Whole Wheat Bread

Steps

  1. Place materials out on counter in clean space. Put two slices of bread onto a plate.
  2. Use a knife to place mayo onto the bread slices. Proceed to put turkey, pepperjack cheese, lettuce and tomato onto the bread.
  3. Use preferred condiments onto the opposite bread slice before joing the pieces.
  4. Enjoy !! :)
