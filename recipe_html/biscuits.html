Biscuit Recipe

Description

The Biscuits above represent more than an morning breakfast or a tea time snack. The love and care required to make a mouth-watering treat demands respect and time!

Never forget that a Biscuit is a treat, thus it deserves honorable "treatment"

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 1/2 ticks cold unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and line baking sheet with parchment paper. Combine flour,baking powder, and salt in one small bowl. In another bowl combine the milk and honey. Stir contents in seperate bowls till combined
  2. Cut butter into chunks before adding to flour. Seperate butter into size of small peas inside flour. Add milk and honey mixture to flour then mix gently until no dry spots
  3. Flour work surface, pat dough into rectangles, then roll until 1/2 inch thick Fold in thirds, roll, fold, roll, repeat then cut 3-inch biscuits
  4. Place biscuits onto baking sheet Bake for 12-14 minutes until tops are crisply browned
  5. Enjoy!!!
